Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take insurance?

No. We operate outside insurance to keep pricing simple and transparent.

Can I still use insurance?

Yes. You can still use insurance for labs, imaging, medications, specialists, or outside services when needed.

Is this concierge medicine?

No. We provide concierge-level access at community-level pricing.
